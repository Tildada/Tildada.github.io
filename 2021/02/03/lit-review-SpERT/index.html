lit review "Span-based Joint Entity and Relation Extraction with Transformer Pre-training"

Wentao's Blog
2021-02-03
Relation Extraction, lit review

一、Span-based approach

句中任何的一个token字串都有可能是一个实体，即一个span。任一一对span都可能存在某种关系。同BIO标注不同，在BIO标注中，模型给每个token一个标签，所以一个token难以被多个实体共享。span更容易处理实体重叠问题。

二、模型方法

Span Classification:

span’s BERT embeddings combined using max-pooling + width embeddings + cls —> softmax，确定当前span是否是实体，是什么类型实体

Span Filtering:

No beam search , pre-filter spans longer than 10 tokens, limiting the cost of span classification to O(n)

Relation Classification:

localized context representation c，头实体尾位置到尾实体头位置部分token embedding maxpooling

Training:

Dataset: SciERC, CoNLL04, ADE
损失函数为entity classification和relation classification损失函数相加

负采样

Span Classification: a fixed number Ne of random non-entity spans as negative samples.
Relation Classification: ground truth作为正样本，取其中实体并组合以不存在的关系(strong)，得到Nr个负样本。这样的设计要比随机span pair采样(weak)作为实体效果更好。
训练时为每个句子采样，且每个句子只通过bert一次，多个正负样本分别通过一个浅层线性层进行实体关系分类，模型更轻便且加快了训练速度。

三、分析结论

负采样

Span Classification: 负采样样本数多，f1 score高，但在其在样本数超过一定值后趋于稳定。
Relation Classification: Strong比Weak更好，且在使用weak时模型更倾向于预测出实体的subspans，而非整个实体。

Localized Context

对比Full context（all tokens + max pooling）和 Cls token，Localized context表现更好，且在token长度大于50时更加明显。让模型知道“输入句子中哪些部分相关”是重要的。

Pre-training and Entity Representation

Full: fully pre-trained BERT
-Layers: retain pre-trained token embeddings but train the layers from scratch
-Layers,Embeddings: train layers and token embeddings from scratch
Conclusion: Pre-training LM is vital

Entity Representation ==> Max pooling / Sum / Average? Max pooling is the best.

Error Inspection

  1. Incorrect spans
  2. Syntax: the prediction of a relation between two entities, which could possibly be related based on their entity types, but are not related in the sentence.
  3. Logical: Sometimes, a relation is not explicitly stated in the sentence, but can logically be inferred based on the context.
  4. Classification: SpERT correctly predicted two related entities, but assigned a wrong relation type.
  5. Missing annotation: A correct prediction is missing in the ground truth.
